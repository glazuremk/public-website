May 11, 2023. Skopje, Macedonia

Event Info

Organized Globally, Held Locally
Global Azure is from the community for the community event, with local events happening around the world covering Microsoft Azure services. During this FREE global event, communities worldwide will be organizing local editions, and on the 11th of May 2023 it will be organized for the first time in Skopje, North Macedonia. Skopje local in-person event will be organized with total of 6 sessions divided in 2 tracks, starting from 17:00 CET in "Public Room" venue in Skopje. Our Speakers, proven professionals and experts, will cover interesting topics about Microsoft Azure cloud platform, its services and about best practices.
